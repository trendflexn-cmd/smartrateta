❓ How Tax Estimation Works

It estimates your income tax liability based on your annual income and the tax regime you choose. It shows the gross income, tax amount, take‑home income, and effective tax rate.

Enter your total annual income, select your age category (for old regime exemptions), and pick either the new or old tax regime. Click Calculate to see the breakdown.

New Regime offers lower tax rates but removes most deductions and exemptions. Old Regime has higher rates but allows deductions like 80C, HRA, etc. This calculator gives a basic estimate; for exact calculations, consult a tax professional.
