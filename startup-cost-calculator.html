❓ How Startup Cost Estimation Works

It totals your initial investment by adding up several common startup cost categories: equipment, inventory, property/lease, permits, marketing, and professional services.

Enter the estimated cost for each category. You can leave any field as zero if it doesn’t apply. Click Calculate to see your total startup cost.

This calculator covers the most common startup costs. If you have extra items, you can add them to one of the existing fields (e.g., “Professional Services”) or simply note them separately.

It’s an estimate based on the numbers you provide. Always include a buffer (10‑20%) for unforeseen expenses and check actual quotes from suppliers.

Total Startup Cost = Equipment + Inventory + Property & Lease + Permits & Licenses + Marketing + Professional Services
