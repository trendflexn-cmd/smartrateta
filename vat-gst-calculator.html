❓ How VAT/GST Calculation Works

It calculates the GST (or VAT) amount and the total price after tax. You enter the base amount (before tax) and select the GST rate (0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, or 28%).

Enter the base amount (the price without tax), then choose the applicable GST rate. Click Calculate to see the GST amount and the final total (base + GST).

Yes. Although labelled GST, it works for any percentage‑based tax. Just select the rate that matches your country’s tax.

This version calculates GST on the base amount. If you need to extract GST from a tax‑inclusive price, you would need a reverse calculator. You can still do it manually: divide the inclusive amount by (1 + rate/100).

GST = Base Amount × (GST Rate / 100)
Total with GST = Base Amount + GST
