❓ How Profit Margin Works

It calculates your profit amount, profit margin (as a percentage of cost), and markup percentage (as a percentage of selling price) based on your cost price and selling price.

Enter the cost price of your product and the price at which you sell it. The calculator instantly shows the profit and two common profitability ratios.

Profit Margin is the profit expressed as a percentage of the cost price. Markup is the profit expressed as a percentage of the selling price. They measure profitability from different angles.

Absolutely. Just replace "cost price" with the cost to provide the service and "selling price" with what you charge the customer.

Profit = Selling Price − Cost Price
Profit Margin % = (Profit / Cost Price) × 100
Markup % = (Profit / Selling Price) × 100
