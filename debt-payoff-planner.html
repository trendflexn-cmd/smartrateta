❓ How Debt Payoff Planning Works

It tells you how long it will take to pay off a debt if you make fixed monthly payments, while interest continues to accrue. You'll see the total months, total interest paid, and the total amount repaid.

Enter your total debt amount, the monthly amount you can pay, and the annual interest rate. Click "Calculate" to see your payoff timeline.

If your payment doesn’t cover the monthly interest, the debt will never be paid off. The calculator will alert you to increase your payment.

Months = log(Payment / (Payment – Debt × (Rate/12))) / log(1 + Rate/12)
This assumes fixed monthly payments and a constant interest rate.
