❓ How Transfer Fee Calculation Works

It calculates the fee you'll pay on a money transfer and the net amount the recipient will receive. You can choose between a fixed fee, a percentage fee, or a combination of both.

Enter the transfer amount, select the fee type (fixed, percentage, or combined), and fill in the fee amount/percentage. The calculator shows the amount sent, the fee deducted, and the final amount received.

Fixed: A flat fee (e.g., ₹50) regardless of the transfer amount.
Percentage: A fee based on a percentage of the transfer amount (e.g., 1.5%).
Combined: Both a fixed fee plus a percentage fee.

No. This calculator only handles the explicit fee charged by the transfer service. If you're sending money internationally, exchange rate margins may also apply – use our Currency Converter for exchange rate estimates.

Fixed: Fee = Fixed Amount
Percentage: Fee = Transfer Amount × (Fee% / 100)
Combined: Fee = Fixed Amount + (Transfer Amount × Fee%)
Amount Received = Transfer Amount – Fee
