❓ How Savings Goal Tracking Works

It tells you how long it will take to reach a specific savings target, starting from your current savings and adding a fixed monthly contribution, while earning a constant annual return.

Enter the amount you want to save (Target), the amount you already have (Current Savings), how much you'll save each month, and the expected annual return. The calculator shows the time needed in years/months and the final amount accumulated.

The calculator will tell you that you've already reached your goal, and show your current savings as the final amount.
