❓ How Currency Conversion Works

Enter the amount, choose the currency you have (From) and the currency you want (To). The converter instantly shows the converted amount using the latest exchange rates.

Rates are fetched automatically from reliable, free APIs and updated regularly. If the API is unavailable, built‑in fallback rates ensure the converter always works.
