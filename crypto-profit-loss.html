❓ How Crypto Profit/Loss Works

It calculates your net profit or loss from a cryptocurrency trade. You enter the number of coins, buy price, sell price, and any transaction fees. The result shows your total profit/loss and the return percentage.

Fill in the quantity (coins bought), the buy price per coin, the sell price per coin, and the transaction fee percentage (e.g., 1% for exchange fees). Click "Calculate" to see the outcome.

Yes. The calculator subtracts the fee percentage from your total sale value before computing profit/loss, so you see the actual net result.

Profit = (Sell Price × Quantity) − (Buy Price × Quantity) − Fees
Where Fees = (Sell Price × Quantity) × (Fee% / 100).
