Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2024

1. Introduction

Smart Rate ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the smartrate.com website (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

2. Information Collection and Use

We collect limited information only when you choose to provide it:

  • Email address when you subscribe to our newsletter
  • Contact information when you use our contact form
  • Calculator inputs (stored locally on your device only)
  • Non-personal data like browser type and pages visited

3. Local Storage

Smart Rate uses browser localStorage to save your preferences (such as dark mode selection) and exchange rate data. This data is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers without your consent.

4. Third-Party Services

We use exchangerate-api.com to fetch real-time exchange rates. Please review their privacy policy for information about how they handle data.

5. Security

The security of your personal data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@smartrate.com
