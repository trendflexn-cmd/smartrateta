❓ How Invoice Estimation Works

It helps you estimate the total amount of an invoice by calculating the subtotal, applying any discount, adding GST (or any tax), and showing the final invoice total.

Enter the quantity of items/services, the unit price, any discount percentage you want to offer, and the tax rate (e.g., 18% GST). Click Calculate to see the breakdown.

The discount is applied first to the subtotal, and then GST is calculated on the discounted amount. This is the standard practice in most billing systems.

The calculator uses the term "GST" but you can treat it as any tax or surcharge. Just enter the appropriate percentage.

Subtotal = Quantity × Unit Price
After Discount = Subtotal − (Subtotal × Discount%)
GST = After Discount × (GST%)
Total Invoice = After Discount + GST
