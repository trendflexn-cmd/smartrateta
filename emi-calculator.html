Frequently Asked Questions

EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment. It's a fixed payment you make every month to pay off a loan over a specified period.

EMI = [P × r × (1 + r)^n] / [(1 + r)^n - 1], where P is principal, r is monthly interest rate, and n is number of months.

Yes, longer tenure results in lower EMI but higher total interest paid. Shorter tenure increases EMI but saves on interest.
