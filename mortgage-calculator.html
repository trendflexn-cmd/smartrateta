❓ How Mortgage Calculation Works

It estimates your monthly home loan EMI, total interest, and overall payment based on the home price, down payment, interest rate, and loan tenure.

Enter the full price of the home, the down payment you can make, the expected interest rate, and how many years you want to repay. The calculator automatically computes the loan amount (price minus down payment) and the monthly EMI.

The home price is the total cost of the property. The loan amount is what you actually borrow after paying the down payment. For example, a ₹50 lakh home with a ₹10 lakh down payment results in a ₹40 lakh loan.

No. This calculator only shows principal and interest components of the EMI. Real‑world mortgage payments often include property taxes, home insurance, and other fees, which would increase the monthly outflow.

EMI = P × r × (1+r)n / ((1+r)n − 1)
Where P = loan amount, r = monthly interest rate (annual rate ÷ 12), n = total months (tenure in years × 12).
