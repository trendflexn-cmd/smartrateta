❓ How Salary Breakdown Works

It breaks down your monthly salary by components — Basic, DA, HRA, Bonus — and shows your Gross Salary, Net Salary (after standard deductions), and final Take‑Home pay.

Enter your Basic salary, Dearness Allowance, House Rent Allowance, any bonus or other allowances, and total monthly deductions (like Provident Fund, professional tax). Click Calculate to see the detailed breakdown.

Gross Salary = Basic + DA + HRA + Bonus (all earnings before deductions).
Net Salary = Gross minus deductions like PF, taxes.
In‑Hand / Take‑Home is what you actually receive in your bank account.

The calculator works with the numbers you provide. It doesn’t automatically apply country‑specific tax rules, so you can manually enter your deductions. For exact tax liability, use our Tax Estimator.

Gross Salary = Basic + DA + HRA + Bonus
Net Salary = Gross – Deductions
In‑Hand Salary = Net Salary (assuming no other hidden cuts).
